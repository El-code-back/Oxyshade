# OxyShade Brief

OxyShade is a modular demonstration photobioreactor with Chlorella microalgae. The first version is designed for interiors, educational spaces, pilot installations and partnership conversations in Kyrgyzstan.

Current stage: prototype development and testing.

Primary goal: find pilot sites and partners for measurement, assembly, installation and validation.

Important communication rule: OxyShade should not be described as a certified air purifier or compared with trees until the team has its own measurements and a clear methodology.
